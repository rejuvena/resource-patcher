A powerful mod to enhance Terraria's resource packs.

Resource Patcher serves to accomplish two goals:
1. enhance the Vanilla resource pack system, enabling greater and finer control over resource replacements;
2. provide a framework for mods to register their own resource replacements as texture packs configurable by the user instead.
